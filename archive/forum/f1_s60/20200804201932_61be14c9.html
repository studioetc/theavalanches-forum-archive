Some more samples that haven't been mentioned here yet:

That buzzing noise at 3:33 in "Summer Crane" is sampled from "Hebrew Melody" by Clara Rockmore.

go to 0:21
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8w6sRjVYkow


The "Can't you hear it, oh can't you hear it?" vocals from the beginning of "Two Hearts in 3/4 Time" are sampled from the song "Rosemary" from the soundtrack to the musical How to Succeed in Business Without Really Trying.

go to 1:41
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fJ_PHxW139A


Those heavy drums at around 2:40 in "Frontier Psychiatrist" may be sampled from "Lover and a Friend" by Eddie Bo.

at the beginning
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aaMZWBNx5Sc


The drums and organ near the end of "Two Hearts in 3/4 Time" are sampled from "Ghost Story" by John Cale.
