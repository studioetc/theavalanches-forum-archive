I know what slackjawdyokel is talking about. Before buying SILY I went ahead and downloaded some tracks, including Radio. That version of Radio had some dialogue at the start of the track and I'm guessing if your album has this dialogue then it's an original:

Guy knocking on a door - Johnny, Johnny come on open up! Johnny, Johnny
Johnny (slurring) - Out of my way I'm watching television
Guy - Are you crazy!? That's the test pattern
Johnny - I like it
---
Doctor - Well there are two things to be done, first of all we put him in the hospital and withdraw all television
Guy - I see
Doctor - And then we can do this [pushes a button]
Guy - Radio! Radio! Radio! Radio! (etc...)

That version of Radio runs about 4:54. I eventually bought SILY on CD here in the US and that whole dialogue was missing from Radio.
