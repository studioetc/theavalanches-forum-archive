goldfish wrote:

What Mac player? iScrobbler works fine for me, or is there more to it than that...?

Simon


iScrobbler is the plugin that tells last.fm what you listen to, but yes, there's more to it - once last has a profile for you, it can make a "radio station" (stream) based on your profile. To listen to this, you now need to download player software. It used to be possible to listen with iTunes or whatever your mp3 player is, but now you need the player software, and they're still working the kinks out of the mac version. Alternatively, if you're running Konfabulator, there's a last.fm widget that can play the stream.
