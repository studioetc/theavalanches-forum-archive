don't know the hotel itself riko, but the area is a very central location & very safe - you'll have no trouble getting around transport wise.

Basically everywhere you're planning to go is scattered around (as is everything in london!) so you'll probably be using tubes/buses/cabs to get back from gigs (tubes stop runnning about 12/1am), but that's an inevitable part of being in london. Pub/bar wise the best things are in the east end (shorditch area) but it's not the nicest area to stay.

i'm sure myself, simon & viv will be more than happy to help show you both around & meet up for a few outings! let us know your dates & i'lll check out if there's anything else on that you might like...
