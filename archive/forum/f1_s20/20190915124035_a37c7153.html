the mediafire link title says saint jeromes in 2005 - saint jeromes is a bar in melbourne and since 2005 they started holding a laneway party (which in later years has grown into a kind of travelling music festival in other australian cities). i'm pretty sure there were saint jeromes laneway parties for years before this, but they weren't promoted as such and they certainly weren't anywhere apart from melbourne.
