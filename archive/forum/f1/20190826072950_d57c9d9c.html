That beautiful woodwind section on track 15 (summer crane) which kicks in at 4:04 til roughly 4:34 is from `morning (peer gynt suite no.1) Op.46` also known as `morning suite` from Henrik Ibsens opera `Peer Gynt`(1867). Not sure whos recording it is but i have the one by geoff love and his orchestra which is pretty damn close. recordings of operas or classical dont really stray from the original unless youre walter murphy in which case..hey beethovens fifth is some funky ass shit! laters.
