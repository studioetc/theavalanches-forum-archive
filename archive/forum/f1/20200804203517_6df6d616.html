I genuinely thought 2009 was going to be it, but I don't really have insider info, just educated guesses! Consider the following:

1) Modular were supposed to recieve the finished album December 2008.

2) "Since I Left You" was complete for approx 12 - 18 months before it was released.

3) "Since I Left You" was most successful in UK/Europe, where it was marketed as a sort of 'soundtrack to summer.'



See what I'm getting at? The record has the best chance of success if it's released in the northern summer. It was supposedly complete last December. So I think July 2010 is the earliest likely release date.

Remember too, bands are like the rest of us. Families, deaths, births, sickness, health. There's surely some legitimate reasons for the wait. Sally Seltmann has a new album coming out too.

One thing I am absolutely sure of: complaints about lack of info don't speed up the process ... the instant it's public we'll know about it. And finally, online opinions about the status of the band or its record deserve not even a scrap of credence!!! I know that's completely hypocritcal of me be, but come on - "They've split"?? "They're no good without Dexta"?? "It'll never come out because of copyright"?? give me a freakin break.
