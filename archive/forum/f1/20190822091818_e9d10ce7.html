i recently re-ripped &/or re-encoded all me avalanches mixes, in the same manner as i did me jelly mixes

so, theres now [was] an avalanches mixes d/l page in the swamp [no longer available so ive updated the mix titles to links at divshare. theres also a bunch ov mediafire links further down]. these are not everythins out there, mostly jus the stuff iv either got meself or recorded meself. all now 320kbs, cept fer d/ls offa avs site & stuff from other sources. needless to say, better quality all around. heres a few bitz ov info to accompany em...

jjj mix-up 1: part 1 part 2 part 3 - dexter. got this offa mate on 3xcdr. i believe ittza fm b/cast, since i dont think there woz any other way to listen to jjj back then. he initially sent me mp3 @192kbs, which iz wot i shared even after he sent me 3xcdr. i wudda whacked all 3 bitz togevver this time, but theres ikkle edits/fades, so i didnt bovver

gimix - robbie. originally ripped offa cdr ov emi edition. this time, ripped offa 2xcd promo wiv radio on other cd

at home 1 - d/l from avalanches website in dayz ov yore

breezeblock - 2nd bb appearance, 1st bein edit ov gimix & 3rd bein at home 2

xfm one night stand - robbie & james. d/l from avs website

ammo city - robbie & darren. interview & records

ibiza - robbie & dexter. i fergot boutz this at the time, but fortunately a mate nabbed it offa fm onto tape

one world - james

cornerstone - robbie & dexter. mate in usa sent me cdr ov disc 2

at home 2 - 3rd breezeblock & beats in space. this iz cdr rip, but also originally d/l offa avs site @192kbs

morning becomes eclectic: part 1 part 2 - robbie & dexter. real player rec. i had the chance to getta fm copy ov this but foolishly declined at the time. u still there jason?...

hijack - robbie & dexter

superchunk: part 1 part 2 - re-b/cast ov edited versions. 1st mix iz superchunk march 2001. unfortunately, i didnt rec this at the time ov original b/cast. did d/l a very poor quality mp3. this set iz missin 1st couple & last trax. 2nd mix iz edited version ov gimix

jjj mix-up 3 - james. 2nd mix-up aint werth bovverin wiv cos itz jus a couple of the other mixes thrown togevver by robbie

when i met you - bootleg edited version ov gimix (itz longer but wiv all same trax). i hav changed sum ov trak titles in line wiv currently accepted version & correctin coupla obvious errors. original traxlist

beneath the surface - james. offa cdr maze sent over

pbs, the breakdown - james

stylin - james. straightup.com.au webcast

stylin interview - james. straightup.com.au webcast

next 4 are all still available fer d/l offa avs site: brains teazer, some people, relaxation tape, brains party @ st. jeromes, but who knows how long thatll be the case...

henry chinaski - sleepy bedtime mix for young ones - robbie

lord fitness - miracle device - darren

big tent set 2006 - robbie & tony


all traxlists in the swamp


coupla lives:

kup day kaos live 1997

jjj oz music show live 1997

theres also sum other stuff, most ov which woz previously d/ls offa avs site in the dim & distant past:

brains 3 quicktime

live footage 2004 music quicktime

update 1 jan 2004

update 2 feb 2004

update 3 mar 2004

jjj interview feb 2005

ebtg vs. royksopp (labelled as "individual no. 1" on site)

ray of zdarlight

if theres any problems wiv links, lemme know here. anywayz, enjoy
