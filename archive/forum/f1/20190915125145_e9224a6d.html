The avalanches and meredith..my two favourite things rolled into one, or so I thought. The lead up to mezza saw me as excited as a kid who still believes in santa. But it all went terribly wrong. What the hell were all those dudes doing on stage during the avalanches set - the ones in cheesy costumes, dancing in a half-arsed way? If I want to see people doing lame dancing, talking amongst themselves and drinking beer, I can look around the crowd. Hey, I can look at myself. Why the avalanches think they need to invite their mates on stage is beyond me. If they wanted to have their own private party, they could've waited til after meredith. They were there to entertain the punters, who were hanging to see them. Instead, we got a lame-arsed display of self-indulgence. I've been betrayed.

Gia

I don't know what the go was there either.

I've seen 5000 or so people up at meredith way beyond sensible hours after many hours on the juice, go nuts in the past to robbie chater, and dexta, shook daily and de la cruz, but their set this year at meredith just didn't fit.

This year the crowd started to go nuts, and tried to get into it, but then ended up kinda watching and tapping their feet as the set went on. Not only this, but the crew up on stage started dancing their arses off, but then kinda just milled around as the set wore on as well

was all a little odd

I was so very very excited to see the avs do their thing again, but from a strong start, the set just didn't hold up. A few glimpses of gold towards the end, but most of the crowd had headed back to the tents by then

The next men who followed were not as fluid as I hoped, and had two quite annoying mcs out front, but their music selection at least was strong, and had the remaining crowd a little more engaged.

I'm glad the set at the carpark was good, cos their meredith set wasn't anywhere near as good as their previous efforts.
