Hey Simon,
I know you've only made the error because of your super-keeness to help out people on this board

But Undersea Community is in Japanese. the rapper's name is Manabu Etoh, who was in Alarm115 - the "Japanese-punk inspired" band that became The Avalanches (this was in 1995.) Also, Alarm115 were named after a Japanese personal alarm. Manabu was in the band until his passport expired not long after recording Undersea Community in 1996 at RMIT studios ... and i don't think the Avs have heard from him since.

Sorry, i can't quote anything online, the only mention i could find was in French!

The SILY sleeve thanks Manabu and Namiko Etoh, but also thanks "Katsumi Konjo for Undersea Community" but I can't remember who that peson is. Possibly someone who inspired the song, or later translated it for the band (who had no idea wha the lyrics were about)

Love Penny.
