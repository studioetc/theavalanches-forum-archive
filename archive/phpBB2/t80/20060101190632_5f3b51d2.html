Friday 16th: Rubys Lounge, Belgrave
Sat 17th: Fringe bar, Racecourse Road Flemington


Before heading off to play some shows in UK & NZ Dexter is coming to a
bar near you to present his Sinner Man night ... a night of soul, hip
hop, ragga, RnB, Latin ...

Dexter's sets, are famously known for dropping the old with the new, the
classics with the obscure, the weird with the decidedly whacky, each
record seamlessly forced in to the next, creating a build up that can
only be released on the dancefloor. Cleverly inventing an atmosphere in
a way only he knows how, his ability to bring soul, originality &
happiness to the dancefloor is his only aim and is cherished by
thousands of punters throughout the world.

To celebrate and say goodbye, he will be playing next weekend at Ruby's,
Belgrave on Friday 16th, or in Flemington at the Fringe Bar on Saturday
17th September, with long time turntablist friends Kuya and Ooi.
