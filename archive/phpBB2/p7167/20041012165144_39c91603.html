I caught an interview with Shook Daily this afternoon, I managed to tape record the second mic break, which featured all three members.

They were talking up their Street Ball (basketball) gig at the metro this Friday night. IS ANYONE going? Please, take a minidisc recorder or something! It's been ages since i've heard anything fresh and these guys cut it up with two turntables EACH.

BUT, of most interest to Avalanches scholars such as myself, was the first mic break, in which Dexter spoke about a remix he has just done of the Beastie Boys' single Triple Double. They played the track, but i'm not sure what format its being released on.

Dexter said he produced the track with GORDON McQUILTON, yes, the Ex-Avalanche. Hmmmmmmmmm adding more fuel to my own personal fire of theory that there is some sort of sanctions going on with the departure of those two from the band.

does it make sense? shed some light on the subject everyone.
