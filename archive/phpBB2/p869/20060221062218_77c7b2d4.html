Also,
Those outside of Australia might not realise that "Bobbydazzler" was a series of compilation records released in Australia in the 70s.

You know, compilations of the latest chart hits or whatever. Usually with incredibly ugly album sleeves, and photos of the pop artists on the back.
But yes, Bobby=Rob, Dazzler=Darren, and Bobbydazzler= The Avalanches' producing pseudonym.

Yeah Brains is their club night which started at St Jeromes in Melbourne a couple of years ago.

In the north of the UK, if someone's "a reet Bobby Dazzler" it means they're either really spectacular, or a show-off. UK TV "personality" and arch-twat, David Dickenson, counts it as one of his idiotic catchphrases - along with "it's as cheap as chips".


Wikipedia says:

"Bobby Dazzler was a 1977 Australian television sitcom produced by Crawford Productions starring singer John Farnham as the title character: up and coming pop music star Bobby Farrell. The other regular cast members were Maurie Fields as Bobby's father Fred, an old vaudeville performer; and Olivia Hamnett as Bobby's officious manager Della McDermott."
