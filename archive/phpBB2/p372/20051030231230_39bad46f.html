Perth is really good but clubs are a bit few and far between.....you really want to go to the 'Flying Scotsman' for good Friday nights out, it may be a short taxi ride but is well worth it.

The Amplifier is the best place in Perth by far, always got good bands on but is really hard to find , i asked about 6 taxi drivers and they all said "where".

It used to be called the 'monkey bar' and most people know it as that rather than the amplifier.

The nightlife is good and there is lots of free magazines in Perth that state all the listings for the area, you really wont struggle to find a good night out every day of the week.

A must see is also 'Freemantle' about a half hour bus journey and its got tons of lovely bars, markets and place to eat and stay.

Hope this helps.
