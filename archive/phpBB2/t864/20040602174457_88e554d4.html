Yeah, discobar galaxie isn't bad at all. I saw them 2 times.

It were two very different gigs.

The first time I saw them was in Leuven, on a festival called Marktrock. It was a gig where the frontman, Adriaan Van Hoof, was speaking and singing and yelling at the crowd all the time, which was so cool.

...With all the good things, there is always a BUT:

But the second time I saw them, I was shocked. This time it was in Antwerp, in some club called The Cinema. Before Discobar Galaxie played some other Dj, and at the end of his set, he played all the good songs, I mean the really good crowd-going-wild-songs, that Discobar Galaxie always plays. And although I was shaking my ass off, I had this double feeling; Ok, the songs from this Dj weren't too bad, but he was just screwing it up for Discobar G!

So then came the Discobar. This time it was just a Dj set. No frontman singing and yelling at the crowd, nothing. The opposite of what I expected. I think their charm and class is this contact with the crowd. It turned out to be a lazy set with no feeling at all, no vibes, etc. On top of that the sound was so lousy, I went home about an hour.


So, Wheels, are you going to see them in Aarschot?

Let me know how it has been!
Enjoy them and have a great evening
