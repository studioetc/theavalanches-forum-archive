You've probably not heard them cause their not a band!

optimo is a club night that is on every sunday at the sub club in glasgow scotland and has been running for about past 3-5 years. despite being a sunday night the club it packed every week

its 2 djs twich and wilkes that run the club and dj. they sometimes have bands in as well. they introduced franz ferdinand and the rapture and more recently sons and daughters

they are kind of similar to the avalanaches in that they will play any style of music but as someone has meantioned the tunes they play can be a bit more obscure. I would say but they are probably my fave djs. i ve not heard their cd yet (its not out in scotland till monday) but if it comes close to capturing the club in full flow it should be pretty amazing

their website is www.optimo.co.uk and they ve been putting mixes up every month for you to check out
