Talking of super second-hand finds - as you all are! - my findest was @ a place here called 'The Bower Bird". About 4 years ago the old owner had just received a batch odf old records that was yet to priced and sold.

In a very Shadow-esque moment, a friend and me asked him ever-so-politely if would could venture into that room to take sneaky peek. Anyway, to cut the long story short, I got it for $7.00(Aust), and didn't know it is one of thee most sought after records for headz who know! I have other stories like this, but htis was the penultimate find for me.
