1. Lyrics Born - Later That Day (What can I say, Lyrics Born is my boy. I'd been waiting for this album to drop for so long, and it was worth the weight. Lyrics Born is my generations George Clinton; crazy production skills, by far the best lyricist, and songwriting to make you bob your head)

2. Non-Prophets - Hope (This album is a work of art that can be appreciated by underground and mainstream hip-hop fans)

3. Postal Service - Give Up (I was very suprised by the album, very thick and melodic indie geek rock)

4. Ralph Meyers & Jack Herren Band - Special Album (wonderful. period)

5. Electric 6 - Fire (I can't tell if they are making fun of 80s hair metal, or they actually do love this shit. Either way its the rock album of the year.)

6. Ben Lee - Hey You, Yes You (Dan the Automater delivers a great back drop to one of best singer/songwriters out there)

Honorable Mention:
Soulposition - 8 Million Stories (great follow up to the Unlimited EP)

Justin Timberlake - Justified (no really... im dead serious... check it out)

Johnny Cash - American IV: The Man Comes Around (Hurt is the best cover... ever)

Kill Bill Soundtrack (Thank you RZA and Quentin for re-introducing me to Tomoyasu Hotei, someone I'd long forgottensince the Fear and Loathing soundtrack. BTW this move fucking ROCKS! BEST MOVIE OF THE YEAR!)
