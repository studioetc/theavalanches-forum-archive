Hope it's OK to spam the board with this.

It is with much pleasure I announce that a 12-inch single featuring "Whistler's Delight" and "P-Funk is Playing at My House" is available beginning today.

It is a newly updated version of "Whistler's Delight," with dozens of new samples. Meanwhile, "P-Funk is Playing at My House" features the vocal stylings of Katie Enlow and Gerald A.

The record was released today by England-based Prank Monkey Records and is available in record shops in London, as well as through various online retailers, including www.piccadillyrecords.co.uk.

You can listen to streaming versions of both tracks through this page:
http://www.culturedeluxe.co.uk/prankmonkey/prm003.html.

Check the "News" section at www.djriko.com for an updated list of stores carrying the record. If your local shop doesn't have it, feel free to tell the kind clerk you'd like the store to get it, that it's on Prank Monkey, that the catalog number is PM0003 and the distributor is Shellshock.

This is a limited pressing of 500 copies, so don't delay if you plan to buy one.
