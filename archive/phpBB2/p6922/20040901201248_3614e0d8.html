Dogs Die In Hot Cars - Godhopping!
Neil Ardley - A Symphony of Amaranths
Fennesz - Venice
Ween - Live in Chicago (DVD)
TISM - The White Albun
The Go! Team - thunder lightning crash
Various - Blue Brazil Volume 2
CentralDeliBand - May Contain Traces of Nut

Is it just me? I think Godhopping has such a great chorus! and it's very different from anything happening here in Australia at the moment.

Anyway ... This Fennesz character is interesting, electronic experiment-type stuff. TISM are a silly garage-rock/techno hybrid from australia who cop a lot of shit, but I think they're hilarious.. and CentralDeliBand = ahem, um, my band.

As for the Go Team album!! Listening now, crikey it's a bit short isn't it. The sense of fun and melody reminds me of Junior Senior. Tracks 5 and 8 have stood out for me so far, but as an album it certainly doesn't approach the sort of Avalanches genius i was raving about when I first heard "ladyflash." The production is interesting, theres a healthy dollop of forgotten-tv-themes nostalgia, lots of live instrumentation, but as for a classic SONG per se, you are left kind of wanting. it DOES all end up sounding the same, almost as if they found the one cheerleading record and used it for every single sample. Still, better than most of the junk filling Aussie airwaves at the moment. I'm gonna say 3 Stars. what do you guys reckon?


Have you all heard of this backini character? I don't know how/why/where i got the song, but it sounds kind of like Lemon Jelly.

Captain Bullant

P.S. Am i the only one sick of this 'the avalanches will never be back' bullshit? It was ALWAYS going to be a huge wait for their next record. They haven't 'died' or 'forgotten' anything ... they work slowly, fuck around making silly videos and break their limbs. But they will come back. We're closer than ever, as the modular website suggests. and you know their new album will push yet another envelope. so stop doubting!
